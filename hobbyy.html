My Hobbies

Passion Beyond Studies

Amirul Haziq bin Khalid

My hobbies include playing football and futsal, as well as hiking. These activities help me stay physically active and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Playing football and futsal allows me to release stress after daily routines as a student, while hiking gives me the opportunity to enjoy nature and improve my physical endurance.

Football

Football is more than just a game to me. It's a passion that teaches teamwork, strategy, and discipline. Playing football helps me stay fit and builds camaraderie with teammates.

Futsal

Futsal is an exciting indoor version of football that demands quick thinking, agility, and precise ball control. It's a fantastic way to improve technical skills in a fast-paced environment.

Hiking

Hiking connects me with nature and provides a peaceful escape from daily life. It challenges my physical limits while allowing me to appreciate the beauty of the natural world.

Life Skills Learned

Teamwork

Football and futsal require strong cooperation among teammates to achieve common goals.

Discipline & Patience

Hiking teaches me patience, self-discipline, and mental strength when facing challenges.

Communication

Effective communication on the field leads to better coordination and successful plays.

Physical Health

Regular activity improves cardiovascular health, strength, and overall fitness.

Overall Impact

Overall, my involvement in football, futsal, and hiking has helped me develop positive attitudes such as perseverance and determination.

I believe that these activities contribute to building a balanced character, improving my well-being, and supporting my personal and academic development.
