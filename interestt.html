Passions That Enrich My Life

Amirul Haziq bin Khalid

Beyond my studies and daily routines, I have interests that bring joy, relaxation, and inspiration to my life. These passions help me maintain balance and find happiness in simple pleasures.

Watching Football Matches

One of my main interests is watching football, especially matches in the Malaysia Super League. My favorite team is Selangor FC, a club that is well known for its rich history, passionate supporters, and strong fighting spirit. I enjoy watching Selangor FC because they always show determination and teamwork on the field, regardless of the challenges they face. Following their matches helps me relax and brings me excitement, especially during intense and competitive games. Supporting Selangor FC has also taught me the importance of loyalty, discipline, and perseverance. Through football, I have learned that success comes from consistent effort and unity, values that I admire both in sports and in daily life

Watching football helps me understand game strategies, appreciate teamwork, and feel the excitement of competition. It's not just entertainment - it's a learning experience about dedication and excellence.

90s Malay Sentimental Songs

I also have a great interest in 90s Malay sentimental songs. The captivating melodies and touching lyrics from the golden era of Malay music resonate deeply with me and bring back nostalgic feelings. Another interest of mine is listening to 1990s sentimental music, especially songs by legendary Malaysian bands such as Slam, Ukays, Spring, and Exists. I enjoy these songs because their lyrics are meaningful and emotional, often telling stories about love, heartbreak, and life experiences. The melodies are soothing and timeless, making the songs enjoyable to listen to even today. Listening to 90s jiwang songs helps me relax, reflect on my feelings, and appreciate the depth of classic Malaysian music.
